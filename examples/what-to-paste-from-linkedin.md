# How to Export Your LinkedIn Data for the Resume Builder

Claude cannot access LinkedIn directly — LinkedIn does not have a public API for profile data. Instead, you copy and paste your information manually. This guide shows you exactly what to copy and where to find it.

---

## What to Copy

You need text from five sections of your LinkedIn profile. Open your profile at `linkedin.com/in/your-username` and copy the following:

### 1. About / Summary
Scroll to the "About" section near the top of your profile. Click "see more" if it's truncated. Copy the full text.

### 2. Experience
Scroll to the "Experience" section. For each role, copy:
- Job title
- Company name
- Employment dates (e.g., "Jan 2022 – Present")
- Location
- All bullet points or description text

Click "show more" on each role if the description is truncated.

### 3. Education
Copy each entry including institution name, degree, field of study, and graduation year.

### 4. Skills
Scroll to "Skills" near the bottom of your profile. Click "Show all skills". Copy the full list.

### 5. Certifications (if any)
Scroll to "Licenses & Certifications". Copy each entry including name, issuing organization, and date.

### 6. Honors & Awards (if any)
If you have an "Honors & Awards" section, copy those entries too.

---

## Alternative: Download Your LinkedIn Data Export

LinkedIn lets you download a full data export as a ZIP file. This is more complete but takes 10–24 hours to arrive.

1. Click your profile photo → **Settings & Privacy**
2. Under **Data Privacy** → click **Get a copy of your data**
3. Select **"Want something in particular? Select the data files you're most interested in"**
4. Check: Profile, Positions, Education, Skills, Certifications
5. Click **Request archive**
6. LinkedIn will email you a download link within 10–24 hours

The ZIP will contain CSV files. You can paste the contents of `Profile.csv`, `Positions.csv`, `Education.csv`, and `Skills.csv` directly into Claude.

---

## Tips for Best Results

- The more detail you include per role, the better Claude can write achievement-oriented bullets. Paste everything, even if it feels like too much — Claude will synthesize and trim.
- If your LinkedIn has recommendations, copy those too. Claude will extract any quantified praise (e.g., "increased revenue by 30%") and use it as evidence in your bullets.
- Don't worry about formatting — Claude handles plain text just fine.
